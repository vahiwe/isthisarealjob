How to use this Platform

This platform can be used in verifying job invitatations. The company sentiment analysis scrapes through nairaland comments and runs an analysis on what people are saying about the company
The address verifier makes use of googlemaps api in verifying address.
The invitation text checker checks for grammatical errors in the invitation sent by the company

We also have a Document OCR(Optical Character Recognition) which can scan a picture and extract the text in the picture.

There is a Confidence checker but this can only be accessed after running the Company Analysis, Address Verification and Invitation text checker.
